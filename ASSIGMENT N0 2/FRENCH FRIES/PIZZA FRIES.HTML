PIZZA FRIES

Pizza fries combine the best of both worlds: the comforting indulgence of pizza and the crispy satisfaction of fries. This innovative dish starts with a base of golden, crispy fries, perfectly seasoned to complement the bold flavors to come. On top of this foundation, a generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese blankets the fries, creating a gooey, stretchy layer that binds the toppings together. Next comes the heart of the dish: savory pizza toppings. Traditional marinara sauce, rich with tomatoes and herbs, is spooned generously over the cheesy fries, imparting a tangy sweetness that harmonizes with the saltiness of the cheese. Slices of pepperoni, spicy sausage, or other favorite pizza toppings are scattered across the surface, adding bursts of flavor and texture to each bite. For those who prefer a vegetarian option, mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives can be delightful alternatives. Once assembled, the entire dish is baked until the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned, ensuring that each forkful or bite-sized portion delivers a satisfying crunch and a burst of pizza-inspired taste.
